Privacy Policy for GoAheadSpendIt

Deutsch

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

GoAheadSpendIt is a personal budgeting tool designed to help you track spending patterns. It does not provide financial or investment advice.

Data Collection

GoAheadSpendIt does not collect, track, transmit, or store personal data outside your device. All budget and usage information remains stored locally on your device.

Accounts and Tracking

The app does not require an account and does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking technologies.

Your Control

You have full control over your data. You may delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored information.
